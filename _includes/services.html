Services

The Pedorthic profession includes the design and manufacture of foot orthoses, modification and selection of proper footwear. All aimed to prevent and/or alleviate foot problems. We focus on the fabrication and dispensing of orthotic devices as well as extra depth footwear and custom shoes. Pedorthists accept referrals from Physicians to dispense orthotics for a myriad of lower extremity conditions including:
  • bunions (hallux abducto-valgus)
  • Post operative foot and ankle conditions
  • Sesamoiditis
  • Plantar Fasciitis and heel spurs
  • ACL and Patellofemoral problems
  • Neuroma and metatarsalgia
  • Congenital deformities
Orthotics
The goal of the orthotic is to improve the lower extremity mechanics, by controlling the rear foot, mid tarsal joint, and the forefoot. By controlling the amount and timing of pronation, abnormal forces which lead to chronic overuse symptoms can be alleviated. Orthotic intervention coupled with proper stretching and strengthening lead to tremendous results.
